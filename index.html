We are Recruiting

At IoT Alliance, we believe that the Internet of Things (IoT) is more than just technology- It is a synthesis of creativity, strategic thinking, and technical expertise. IoTA is pillared into three domains: Technical, Corporate and, Design each providing different solutions in different aspects and situations.

Corporate Domain

The Corporate team ensures that club activities run clockwork while fostering relationships and expanding the club’s influence. Their work is essential for the alignment of the club’s efforts with legal standards and broader public engagement.

Sub Domain

Public Relation

The PR team promotes the club’s events and projects, ensuring an emphatic and optimistic public image across social media platforms.
Sponsorships and Finance

The sponsorships and finance team’s focus are secure sponsorships from different clients and manages finances which is crucial for the club’s sustainability. The team works on partnerships that enhance IoT projects while overseeing budgets for the various events, and research.
Events

Sketching and Executing major events here at IoTA is the Events Team’s Vision. They ensure everything runs seamlessly, helping to build rapport with experienced candidates, therefore expanding your social connect.
Editorial and Legal Documentation

This team handles the official communication and documentation, ensuring legal compliance and maintaining the integrity and professionalism of the written content, from reports to social media posts.

Technical Domain

The technical domain is at the core of IoT alliance’s mission - turning ideas into functional IoT systems. The team’s work focuses on cutting-edge technology and
eye-opener solutions. The team also ensures the smooth functioning of the technical aspects of the club.

Sub Domain

AI/ML

The AI/ML subdomain mostly focuses on integrating the world of Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning into Internet of Things to enhance IoT devices, making them smarter and easier to use and more adaptive.
UI/UX

The UI/UX team designs the interactive user interfaces and provides a subtle experience when exploring the IoT world, ensuring the complex systems are easy to use and navigate.
App Development

Building mobile applications to increase the efficiency of using IOT applications. This team ensures connectivity, control, and data accessibility from anywhere.
Web Development

Focusing on building responsive and interactive web platforms, this team creates web interfaces for monitoring and controlling IoT systems. This team also runs the club’s website ensuring a wider reach for the club.

Design Domain

The design team breathes life into the world of IoT by visually representing the ideas and projects. The primary idea is to create an aesthetic that complements and enhances the technical work done in the club.

Sub Domain

Graphic Design

The graphic team creates entrancing posters and graphics that simplify IoT concepts, whether it is for our flagship events, infographics or for digital content.

Video Editing

The primary focus of the video editing team is to convert raw footage of the events, hackathons, workshops into captivating video content. The team enhances footage with the use of VFX and GFX to throw light on the IoT projects.
Photography

Capturing the essence of IoT, this team focuses on documenting our club’s flagship events capturing key moments and core memories, teleporting our viewers into our flamboyant events.
